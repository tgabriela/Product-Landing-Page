Mobility and Flexibility

Get out of the state of comfort and sedentarism. Your body is made to move. Consolidating your mobility and flexibility means regenerating yourself energetically. Flexibility relaxes your muscles and accelerates your blood circulation.

Power

You need strength, speed and power to overcome resistance. Strength training does not mean bodybuilding. It helps you build and maintain your muscle mass. To become stronger! Everything from better sleep, a less stressed mind, more energy, better bone density and faster burning of fat at rest.

Resistance

How is your heart? How is your endurance? Improve your cardiovascular performance through aerobic activity in the target zone of your heart rate. Increasing resistance helps you overcome discomfort or stress more easily. Resistance makes you feel more energetic and full of life.
